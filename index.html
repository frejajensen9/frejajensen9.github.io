About Me

Hello! My name is Freja, and I’m 24 years old. I’m from Copenhagen, Denmark, and recently moved to Hong Kong for a semester as part of finishing my master’s in Computer Science. In my spare time, I’m a big fan of sports, especially volleyball and running, and I enjoy spending time with my friends and family.

I chose to participate in COMP4461 this semester because I’m very interested in the topic of developing technology based on human needs. I want to understand humans better in order to create better software solutions that actually solve real problems and fulfill the needs of users. I have created ‘COMP4461 Projects’page on this website, which serves as a diary of the processes and personal reflections.

This portfolio has been developed based on the minimal theme from https://github.com/orderedlist/minimal
